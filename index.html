    📜 View Guttergories Rules

    The Letter: A random letter is selected at the start of each round. All answers must begin with that letter. Ignore "A", "An", or "The" at the start.

    The Categories: You’ll get a list of funny and dirty prompts. Write an answer for each that starts with the round’s letter.

    The Timer: You must finish before time runs out. No edits afterward!

    Scoring (manual):

    • 🟢 Unique answer: 10 points
    • 🟡 Matching answer: 5 points
    • 🔴 No answer: 0 points
    • 🌟 Alliteration bonus: +1 per matching starting letter (up to 3)
      Example: “Sexy Slippery Snakes” = 10 + 3 = 13 points

    Other Rules:

    • Group voting breaks ties or disputed answers
    • Only one game can run at a time
    • No Googling unless agreed by all players
